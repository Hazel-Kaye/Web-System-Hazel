Cappuccino Coffee

Ingredient Quantity
Espresso 1-2 shots (around 1-2 ounces)
Milk ¾ cup whole milk (preferred) or 2% or skim milk
Milk Frother (optional) —
Sweetener (optional) —
Cocoa powder or ground cinnamon (optional) —

Method:

For a delightful cappuccino, start by brewing 1-2 shots of espresso, or strong coffee in a smaller quantity if you don't have an espresso machine. Next, heat ¾ cup of milk with your preferred method: steam it, heat it in a saucepan, or whisk it vigorously in a pot to create froth. Once the milk is steaming hot, gently pour it over the espresso in a cappuccino mug, holding back the froth with a spoon.expand_more Finally, top your cappuccino with a thick layer of the frothed milk.exclamation Feel free to add sugar, honey, cocoa powder, or cinnamon for an extra touch of sweetness or flavor. Remember, the classic ratio is 1/3 espresso, 1/3 steamed milk, and 1/3 milk foam, but feel free to adjust based on your taste!
