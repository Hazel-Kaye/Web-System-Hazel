Americano Coffee

Ingredient Quantity
Espresso shot(s) 1-2 oz (30-60 ml)
Hot water 8 oz (240 ml)
Cream (optional) —
Sweetener (optional) —

Method:

Brew a shot of espresso using an espresso machine, espresso maker, or Aeropress. Combine the espresso with hot water according to your preferred ratio. Enjoy your smooth, flavorful Americano! Note: A true Americano does not include milk like a latte or cappuccino, but you can add cream or sweetener if desired.
